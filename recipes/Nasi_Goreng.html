Nasi Goreng

Nasi Goreng, Indonesian fried rice. This dish can be enjoyed by itself or as the basis of a larger meal, for example with a rijsttafel.

It is very easy to make and won't take more than 20 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • cooking spray
  • eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 leek, chopped
  • clove garlic, minced
  • green chile peppers, chopped
  • ½ pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • ½ pound peeled and deveined prawns
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3 tablespoons sweet soy sauce (Indonesian kecap manis)
